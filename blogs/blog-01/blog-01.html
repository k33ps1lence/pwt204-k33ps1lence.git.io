What Youâ€™ll Learn in CCNA

The CCNA curriculum is designed to provide hands-on skills and theoretical knowledge. Key topics include:

  • Networking Basics: Understanding how devices communicate in a network and the OSI and TCP/IP models.
  • Switching and Routing: Configuring and troubleshooting switches and routers for small to medium-sized networks.
  • IP Addressing and Subnetting: Learning IPv4 and IPv6, along with efficient IP address management.
  • Security Fundamentals: Basics of securing a network from potential threats.
  • Wireless Networking: Introduction to Wi-Fi networks and their configurations.
  • Network Automation: Learning basic Python scripting and automation tools to manage networks.
